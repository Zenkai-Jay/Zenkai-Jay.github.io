Classic Pizza

A simple yet delicious Italian-style pizza made with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil.

Time: 20 Mins    Serves: 2–3    Difficulty: Easy

This classic pizza recipe is perfect for both beginners and seasoned home cooks. With just a few fresh ingredients and minimal prep, you'll create a flavorful pizza with a crispy crust and perfectly melted cheese.

Ingredients List

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup pizza or marinara sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes or grated parmesan

Tips & Variations

  • For extra crispiness, bake directly on a pizza stone.
  • Swap mozzarella for burrata or provolone for a twist.
  • Add toppings like mushrooms, olives, or pepperoni.
  • Brush the crust edges with olive oil before baking for added flavor.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough:

Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface until it reaches your desired thickness. Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Step 3: Add the Sauce:

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Step 4: Add Toppings:

Layer the mozzarella slices over the sauce. Add tomato slices and season lightly with salt and pepper.

Step 5: Bake:

Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Step 6: Finish & Serve:

Remove from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and top with fresh basil. Slice and serve warm.
